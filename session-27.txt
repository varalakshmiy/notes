pip3.9 install ansible-core

1. tags will not be respected and cascaded by include_role, but import_role will respect the tags
2. import_role parse the playbook before execution, include_role parse the playbook at the time of execution
3. condition applied at import_role applies to all the tasks. include_role role does not care about conditions
4. You cannot use loops on 'import_role' statements. You should use 'include_role' instead
